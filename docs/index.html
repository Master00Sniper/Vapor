How It Works

Three simple steps to a distraction-free gaming experience.

1

Configure Your Preferences

Choose which apps to close, set your volume levels, pick your power plans, and customize temperature alerts. Do this once.

2

Launch Any Steam Game

Vapor detects when you start a game and automatically applies your settings. Apps close gracefully, volumes adjust, and your PC optimizes itself.

3

Quit and Resume

When you're done playing, Vapor restores everything. Closed apps reopen, power settings return to normal, and you get a summary of your session.
